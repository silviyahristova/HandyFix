Services

Electrical services

Whether you require a simple smoke alarm fitting or a full-blown lighting design and installation service, ourprofessional team is available for all jobs, no matter the size or scale. We offer low-cost, high-standard services in and around London. We provide the following electrical services: installing lighting systems, access control systems (including electric gate installation, and remote entry systems), CCTV systems, fire alarms, security alarms, network and telephone cabling, switches and outlet repair, fuse board upgrading, ceiling fan installation, and etc

Plumbing services

We involve a wide range of tasks and services for ensuring the proper functioning of water supply, drainage, and sewage. You can have services such as pipe installation, connecting water heaters and garbage disposals, clearing clogged drains, repairing leaks, and installing water filtration and softening systems to improve water quality. We are providing 24/7 emergency services in urgent situations.

Kitchen and bathroom renovations

We encompass a wide range of tasks related to maintenance, repair, renovation, and installation in these areas at your home. We can install and repair your kitchen cabinets, countertops, tile backsplash, faucet and sink, appliances, fix switches, lights, and outlets, caulking and sealing. We can do the following bathroom installations and repairs: sink, faucet, toilet, shower, and bathtubs, wall and floor tiling; grout, caulk and sealing, cabinets, lights, etc.

Painting and decoration services

Painting and decorating services involve enhancing the interior or exterior of a building. Interior painting involves painting the walls, and ceilings, preparing surfaces, wallpaper installation, wall or ceiling cracks, holes, or any surface imperfections. Exterior services focus on the outer surfaces such as the walls, doors, fences, decks, and more. We use weather-resistant paints and coating to protect against the elements.

Assembly services

We are providing assembly of furniture and equipment. We can assemble desks, chairs, cabinets, outdoor equipment (trampolines, swing sets), gym equipment, office furniture, setting up appliances, and electronics. In addition to assembly, we can disassemble items for moving or storage at a new location.

Smart home installations

We can help make your home smart by installing smart lighting that can be controlled remotely via smartphone or voice commands, a smart thermostat, home security system integration (such as cameras, motion sensors, and smart locks), connecting smart appliances such as refrigerators, ovens, and washing machines to your home network for enhanced control and automation, installing smart blinds and etc.

Windows and blinds services

We can offer you installation on new windows and blinds in your home or office. This includes measuring, fitting, and mounting the windows and blinds. Repairing if there is an issue with the existing ones. We can help you choose the right style, material, and colour to match your decor.

Door services

Door repairs refers to the process of fixing or restoring a door that has become damaged or worn out. Doors service includes hinge repair or replacement, lock and latch repair, frame repair, panel or glass replacement, adjusting door alignment, trimming, painting, and security upgrades such as adding a peephole or reinforcing the strike plate.
